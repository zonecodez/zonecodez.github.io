How would you scale the project?

I would build a site creating wordclouds for the most popular songs of Will Smith, Jaden Smith and Willow Smith using pictures from their instagrams as templates for the clouds.

Why did you choose this particular song?

I chose Icon by Jaden Smith because this was his first major hit and I believe there will be many more to follow. Additionally, I was curious to see what words he used the most in a song that was so repetitive.
